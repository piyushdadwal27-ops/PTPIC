About the Club

Guided by experts.
Driven by students.

The Pharma Tech & Pharmacy Innovation Club is where pharmacy stops being boring and starts becoming exciting. It's a space where you don't just study — you compete, create, and build the career you actually want.

"If you want to enjoy learning, stand out from the crowd, and actually feel prepared for your future — this is where it starts."
Why PTPIC Works
01
Game-Like EngagementPoints, badges, and leaderboards make every session feel rewarding — not another lecture to sit through.
02
Industry-Relevant SkillsEvery program is built around what pharma companies actually hire for — not just what's in the textbook.
03
Real ConnectionsMeet professionals, alumni, and peers who open doors to internships, jobs, and research opportunities.
04
All-Round DevelopmentTechnical depth meets soft skill training — so you graduate confident, not just qualified.

How to Join

Six steps to level up.

Free and open to all pharmacy students — no prior experience required.

01
Enroll on this page
Fill the form, pick your category tracks, and submit — takes under 2 minutes.
02
Join our community
You'll receive a WhatsApp / Discord invite. All communication and resources live there.
03
Attend orientation
Show up to the first session — meet the team, understand how the points system works.
04
Pick your track & earn
Participate in workshops and challenges. Earn XP toward your leaderboard rank.
05
Compete & collaborate
Enter bi-monthly competitions, build projects with peers, engage with industry mentors.
06
Earn your certificate
Complete your track milestones and receive the official PTPIC Certificate of Achievement.
